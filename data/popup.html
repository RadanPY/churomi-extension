Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-10

Churomi provides a browser extension that helps keep webpages active by modifying certain page-visibility and focus signals locally in your browser. This policy explains what information is processed when you use the extension and related services.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address (via Firebase Authentication) so you can sign in.
  • Device identifier: a randomly generated device ID stored in the extension and sent to our API in the x-device-id header to enforce device limits.
  • Device metadata: your user agent and timestamps (e.g., last seen) to help manage devices and security.
  • Membership & billing: subscription status and limited payment metadata (handled by Stripe; we do not store your card number).
  • Support: information you send to us if you contact support.

What we do not collect

  • We do not intentionally collect your browsing history, page content, or form inputs.
  • The “hide activity” behavior is implemented locally inside your browser.

How we use information

  • Provide sign-in, membership checks, and device limit enforcement.
  • Prevent fraud/abuse and secure accounts.
  • Operate billing (via Stripe) and show payment history.

Sharing

We share limited information with service providers to operate Churomi, including Firebase (authentication), Stripe (payments), and our hosting provider (API). We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

Account and device records are retained as needed to provide the service and enforce device limits. You can request deletion by emailing support.
